Roadmap

some of the goals of this exciting project

Development MVP

The game's MVP will feature core mechanics, character development, and a blockchain-based economy with TLOU tokens for asset purchases, resource trading, and secure, transparent transactions.

Game launch

Launch game after testing, target audience marketing. Build community with social media, events, contests, and player engagement. Partnerships with blockchain platforms and exchanges for visibility and adoption.

Refine and update plataform

We will provide ongoing support and expand the game's scope by creating new content, improving performance and scalability, and integrating new blockchain-based features and technologies.

Boost TLOUCrypto community

We will establish a sustainable business model to ensure the game's long-term viability and success by focusing on expanding the community through marketing, events, and partnerships.

TLOUCrypto isn't just a game.

it's also an innovative economy powered by blockchain technology. The game's token, TLOU will be the common currency for transactions and rewards, enabling players to collaborate, compete, and build communities. TLOU tokens will be used to purchase in-game assets, trade resources, and engage in transactions with other players.
